About WalletGuard

WalletGuard was born out of a shared passion for helping individuals achieve financial empowerment and control. Our journey began as a Portfolio Project at Holberton School, where we aimed to create a comprehensive financial management solution that combines usability with powerful features.

This project is a testament to our dedication to creating practical applications that make a positive impact on users' financial lives.
